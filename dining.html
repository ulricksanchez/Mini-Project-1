Asian-Fusion

Our Seaside restaurant is home to one of the first restaurants on Baler specializing in Nikkei cuisine. Created years ago by Japanese immigrants in Peru, Nikkei dishes are bold and balanced with Japanese cooking styles and traditional Peruvian heat.
